About FireSlab

Building Thermal Solutions
That Last.

FireSlab is a brand focused on heating, hot water storage, and thermal system solutions for residential, commercial, and industrial projects.

What started with a simple goal — building reliable and long-lasting systems — has grown into a brand trusted across different applications and industries. From water storage tanks to heat exchangers and insulation systems, our products are designed to perform efficiently in real working conditions.

Our Vision

To build dependable and efficient thermal solutions that combine performance, durability, and practical engineering for modern projects.

Manufacturing Strength

Our products are manufactured using high-quality steel and modern fabrication processes to ensure strength, reliability, and long service life.

  • Precision manufacturing
  • Corrosion resistance
  • Efficient thermal performance

Quality Standards

From material selection to final finishing, every product goes through careful inspection and testing to ensure dependable performance.

We believe products should perform reliably for years in actual site conditions, not just on paper.
